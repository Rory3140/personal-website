About Me

I am an enthusiastic, outgoing, and positive person with an eagerness to learn and embrace new challenges. I have had a passion for computers and technology for as long as I can remember - since learning the basics of Java when I was 13, I have been captivated by the sense of creative freedom and the opportunity for critical thinking that Development and Coding provides. I am also an avid rugby player, having represented my country with Ireland U19 and currently playing for both my university at Division 1 level and the New York Major League Rugby Academy. My experience in rugby has taught me invaluable lessons in teamwork, perseverance, and leadership, all of which I bring to the table when working with others on coding projects.
